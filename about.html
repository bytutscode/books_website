About me

How to learn to write and how to find your reader. About me, my books and my fate.

When asked why I became a writer, I always confidently say: "This is fate."

I really composed stories from childhood, at first I bumped them under my nose, drawing characters in albums (once I was caught by a nanny for this, and I laughed terribly), then writing in notebooks. The second reason is books! I lived in them.

It was happiness – to choose books in the library and dive into them with my head, nobody led my reading, it was haphazard, but constantly. And my mother read out loud for the night, even as a teenager! This is how it all developed gradually, and continues to take shape until now.
